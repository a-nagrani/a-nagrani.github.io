Arsha Nagrani

I am a PhD student supervised by Andrew Zisserman in the VGG group, with a special interest in the intersection of computer vision and speech technology. As of 2018, I am funded by a Google Fellowship.

I am also working part-time at Google Research with Chen Sun and Cordelia Schmid. I did my undergrad at the University of Cambridge, where I worked with Roberto Cipolla and Richard Turner.
